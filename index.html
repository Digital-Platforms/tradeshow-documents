Purpose

This site provides an overview of Digital Platforms tradeshow content at Shared Services Canada (SSC). It brings together project highlights, reference materials, and visual assets that support collaboration and knowledge sharing across teams.

Featured initiatives include 3D Printing and Design, Radio Frequency Identification (RFID), Digital Twins, and Internet of Things (IoT). These projects demonstrate how SSC is exploring and applying emerging technologies to support reliable, secure, and modern digital services in alignment with Government of Canada priorities. It’s a space to connect, learn, and spark innovation across our teams and initiatives.
